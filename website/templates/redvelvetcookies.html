red velvet cookies

Home Cookies

Ingredients

butter

125 g unsalted butter

egg

1 medium egg

brown sugar

100 g light brown sugar

sugar

75 g white granulated sugar

vanilla extract

1 teaspoons vanilla extract

red food colouring

1 teaspoons red food colouring

flour

265 g plain flour

cocoa powder

15 g cocoa powder

white chocolate chips

300 g white chocolate chips

bicarbonate of soda

½ teaspoons bicarbonate of soda

baking soda

1 ½ teaspoons baking soda

salt

½ teaspoons sea salt

how to make

01

Beat butter & sugar

Add butter and sugars to a bowl and beat until creamy.

02

Add remaining ingredients

Add in egg, vanilla and red food colouring and beat again.Then Add in the plain flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, and salt and mix it together until a cookie dough is formed!

03

Add white chocolate chip

Add white chocolate chips and combine until they're distributed well!

04

Divide cookie dough

Weigh your cookies out into eight cookie dough balls - they're about 115-120g each. If you don't want them as big, feel free to make them smaller.

05

Freeze cookies dough

Once they're rolled into balls, put your cookie dough in the freezer for at least 30 minutes, or in the fridge for an hour.

06

Preheat oven

Whilst the cookie dough is chilling, preheat your oven to 180C Fan, or 200C regular.

07

Bake

Take your cookies out of the freezer and put onto a lined baking tray. Bake the cookies in the oven for 12-14 minutes.

08

Let the cookies cool

Once baked, leave them to cool on the tray for at least 30 minutes, as they will continue to bake whilst cooling.
