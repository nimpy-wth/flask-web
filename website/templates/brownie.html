CHOCOLATE BROWNIE CAKE

Home Cake

Ingredients

cocoa powder

2 teaspoons cocoa powder

baking powder

1 Â½ teaspoons baking powder

brown sugar

75 g light brown sugar

vanilla extract

1 teaspoons vanilla extract

butter

125 g butter

eggs

2 eggs

sugar

125 g caster sugar

flour

100 g plain flour

syrup

1 teaspoons golden syrup

chocolate

125 g plain chocolate

how to make

01

Preheat oven

Heat oven to 180C/fan 160C/gas 4. Grease and line a 20cm cake tin.

02

Melting Ingredients

Place the butter, caster sugar, brown sugar, chocolate and golden syrup in the pan and melt gently on a low heat until it is smooth and lump-free. Then remove the pan from the heat.

03

Whisk the eggs

Break the eggs into the bowl and whisk with the fork until light and frothy.

04

Mix Everything

Add the eggs, vanilla extract or essence, flour, baking powder and cocoa powder to the chocolate mixture and mix thoroughly.

05

Bake

Put the mixture into the greased and lined cake tin and place on the middle shelf of the oven. Bake for 25-30 mins.

06

Let the cake cool

Remove and allow to cool for 20-30 mins before cutting into wedges and serving.
