chocolate chip cookies

Home Cookies

Ingredients

butter

1 cup butter, softened

eggs

2 eggs

brown sugar

1 cup packed brown sugar

sugar

1 cup white sugar

vanilla extract

2 teaspoons vanilla extract

flour

3 cups all-purpose flour

chocolate chips

2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

walnuts

1 cup chopped walnuts

hot water

2 teaspoons hot water

baking soda

1 teaspoons baking soda

salt

Â½ teaspoons salt

how to make

01

Gather ingredients

making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.

02

Preheat Oven

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

03

Mix ingredients

Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth. And add eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

04

Dissolve baking soda

Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.

05

Add chocolate chip & walnuts

Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.

06

Bake

Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake in the preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.

07

Let the cookies cool

Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
