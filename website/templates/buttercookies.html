butter cookies

Home Cookies

Ingredients

butter

1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature

eggs

1 large egg, plus 1 large egg yolk for brushing

sugar

2/3 cup granulated sugar

vanilla extract

1 teaspoons vanilla extract

flour

2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

salt

1 teaspoons salt

how to make

01

Preheat Oven

Preheat oven to 375° and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper.

02

Mix ingredients

In a medium bowl, whisk together flour and salt. Using a hand mixer, beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy, 4 to 5 minutes.

03

Add egg & vanilla extract

Add egg and almond or vanilla extract if using and beat until combined.Gradually add the flour mixture and beat until just combined.

04

Dissolve egg yolk

In a small bowl, beat egg yolk with two teaspoons water.

05

Divide dough

Divide dough in half.On a floured surface, roll each dough out evenly to 1/4" thick. Punch out rounds with a 2" cookie cutter

06

Brush tops of cookies with egg wash

Placing them about 1" apart on prepared baking sheets. Use the tines of a fork to create a crosshatch pattern on the cookies, then brush tops of cookies with egg wash.

07

Bake

Bake 10 to 12 minutes, until edges are beginning to turn golden. Cool completely before serving.
