Buttery Cornbread

Home Bread

Ingredients

butter

2/3 cup softened butter

flour

2-1/3 cups all-purpose flour

sugar

1 cup sugar

eggs

3 large eggs

milk

1-2/3 cups 2% milk

cornmeal

1 cup cornmeal

baking powder

4-1/2 teaspoons baking powder

salt

1 teaspoon salt

04

Combine Everything

Combine creamed mixture and egg mixture. Combine the ingredients thoroughly.

05

Bake

Pour into baking pan. Bake for 22-27 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean
